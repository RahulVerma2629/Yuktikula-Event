About Yuktikula
"Where Ideas Meet Innovation"
Yuktikula is the official technical and innovation-driven club under the Department of Computer Science and Data Science (CSDS). Founded with the spirit of collaboration and curiosity, the club serves as a dynamic platform where ideas are cultivated, skills are sharpened, and meaningful projects come to life.

We believe that the intersection of creativity and technology is where the future is built. Whether it’s coding, data analysis, AI, design, research, or product thinking—Yuktikula is a space where every tech enthusiast can thrive. Our community brings together passionate students who are eager to learn beyond the classroom, solve real-world problems, and take initiative.

Through a variety of activities—ranging from technical workshops, seminars, and hackathons to hands-on projects, knowledge-sharing sessions, and design sprints—we create opportunities for members to not only develop their technical toolkit but also grow in leadership, teamwork, and innovation.

Whether you're a beginner curious about coding or an advanced developer looking for your next challenge, Yuktikula welcomes you to be part of something impactful, inclusive, and truly exciting.
